Pumpkin is the first one to join the family. He was a little baby back then, arrived in a board box, with dad and Jen. He still is a baby and never "grew up". Timmy was adopted from a friend of dad. The day she arrived is unforgotten. She was fierce and frightened. hid in the basement for the entire night, Then slowly came out, and slowly stole our hearts, all of them and whole of them! Last one to join was Pluto, yet the one to dominate the house and neighbourhood too.
